Idol Fanfiction

Writing fanfiction has become one of my favorite hobbies, and it all began with my love for Thai and Nex from BUS Because of You I Shine, one of Thailand’s most popular boy bands. Even though many readers have encouraged me to open a donation feature, I choose not to because I feel that receiving money from others isn’t the right thing for me. For me, writing is a passion—not something I want to profit from—and sharing my stories freely makes the experience even more meaningful.

My most popular fanfiction, เตหนี, has gained over 20,000 readers, making it a standout in the fandom. The story follows an engineering student who develops a crush on an older dentistry student and tries various amusing ways to flirt with him. Readers love its humor, charm, and lighthearted storytelling, which has helped the fic spread quickly. I’ve also created TikTok videos to promote it, and through that, I’ve received several trophies and become one of the more well-known writers in the fandom. Many fans even recommend my work on X (Twitter), which always makes me grateful and motivated.

Although I don’t have as much time to write nowadays, my love for fanfiction hasn’t gone away. I still try to find moments to continue writing whenever I can, because creating stories brings me joy, inspiration, and a strong connection to the fandom that supports me.

My TikTok

I started writing fanfiction in 2022, but I only wrote for a few months before stopping. In 2024, I began making TikToks because I was bored—mostly idol content and POV edits about Thai and Nex. While editing those POVs, I started thinking about my own original plot.


Some viewers who watched my POVs became interested in the story and wanted me to turn it into a fanfiction. Because of that, I decided to give writing another try. As more people read it, they began to recognize me for my fanfiction.


I deleted the very first fanfiction I wrote because, looking back, I wasn’t satisfied with it. Still, I kept writing, improving with each story. Over time, I wrote more fanfiction and became more well-known in the fandom.
