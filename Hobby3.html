My Gallery

This website serves as a personal gallery showcasing my drawings and paintings, created mainly in digital form and occasionally on paper. It represents my artistic world, a space where ideas, emotions, memories, and imagination come together through visual expression. Each artwork is a reflection of my creative process, shaped by experimentation, observation, and personal inspiration. Through color, composition, and detail, my work explores mood and meaning, inviting viewers to interpret each piece in their own way and connect with the stories and feelings behind the imagery.

Most of my work is created on an iPad, allowing me to work with flexibility and precision while exploring a wide range of techniques and styles. Alongside my digital pieces, I also create selected works using traditional materials on paper, adding a tactile and expressive quality to my practice. Working across both digital and traditional mediums allows me to develop a versatile artistic approach while maintaining a consistent visual identity.

About my Gallery

I have been studying art since kindergarten, and drawing has been a part of my life from a very young age. What began as a simple interest gradually grew into a deep passion, shaping the way I observe the world and express myself creatively. Over the years, my love for art has continued to grow through constant practice, curiosity, and exploration. Each stage of learning helped me develop my skills, strengthen my artistic voice, and gain a deeper understanding of visual expression.


In 2020, In 2020, I began drawing on an iPad, and it quickly became my preferred medium . This transition opened up new creative possibilities and allowed me to experiment freely with techniques, colors, and styles. I found the digital process both exciting and inspiring, as it offered flexibility while still allowing me to maintain a personal, expressive approach. Since then, digital art has played a major role in my artistic journey, giving me new ways to express ideas and emotions while continuing to grow and evolve as an artist.
